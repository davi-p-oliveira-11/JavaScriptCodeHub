## Average of Two

Create a program that reads a student's two grades in a <br>subject and displays their average on the screen. <br>
Example: <br>
Grade 1: 4.5 <br>
Grade 2: 8.5 <br>
The average between 4.5 and 8.5 is equal to 6.5.
